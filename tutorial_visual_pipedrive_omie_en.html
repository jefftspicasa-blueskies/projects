Visual Registration Tutorial
Pipedrive + Omie

Visual guide for the process between sales, customer registration, and the finance queue. The interface was reorganized with a black visual language focused on readability, contrast, and quick step-by-step navigation.

Overview

The flow starts with deal creation, goes through customer validation, and ends with service order release in Omie. Each card below combines objective guidance and the corresponding screenshot.

SalesOpens the opportunity, fills initial data, and registers the customer correctly in Pipedrive.
RegistrationChecks CUID, IDN, and master data to avoid duplicates or identification errors.
FinanceSends the request to the service order queue, validates creation in Omie, and prepares billing.
Step 1

1. Deals

In this initial phase of registering new deals, we don't have major changes, the flow remains the same.

Goal: start the correct opportunity.
Step 2

2. Final Customer

Extra attention is needed when selecting the final customer. The registration is still being updated, it is necessary to select the customer correctly.

Attention: select the final customer correctly.
Step 3

3. Campaign Type

Below we have the campaign type option, whether by agency or direct client. When agency is selected, use agencies that are registered, don't recreate a duplicate name without being absolutely sure it doesn't exist in the database.

Attention: don't create duplicate agencies.
Step 4

4. Confirm Data

After closing as won, you need to revisit the deal data and confirm that all data from both the customer and the agency (if applicable) are as shown in the PI.

Confirm data matches the PI.
Step 5

5. CUID and IDN

Attention to CUID and IDN creation, these still need to be done manually.

Attention: CUID and IDN done manually.
Step 6

6. Mark CUID and IDN

Same as in the previous image - CUID and IDN still need to be done manually and marked correctly.

Expected result: CUID and IDN marked correctly.
Step 7

7. Billing Queue

When the deal is closed and delivery is marked as (delivered), finance must come to the deal and mark to move to the billing queue. Confirm the PI number and the expected payment date.

Confirm PI number and expected payment date.
Step 8

8. Service Order Created in Omie

After marking the billing queue, a service order will be created in Omie within a few minutes. It then goes to a queue for review and then invoice issuance. After invoice is issued, you need to return to Pipedrive and inform on the deal the invoice number, the invoiced amount, and the invoice payer. After a few minutes the accounts receivable will be generated.

Result: service order created and accounts receivable generated.
